About MyMedia

MyMedia is a simple, personal media player designed to help you organize and enjoy your favorite music and videos from popular platforms like YouTube and Spotify.

Our mission is to provide a seamless and distraction-free listening experience, allowing you to focus on the music you love.

This project is continuously evolving, with new features and improvements planned for the future. Thank you for using MyMedia!

For support or feedback, please contact us at support@mymedia.com.
